Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to create an account to use Facility Finder?

No, you can use Facility Finder without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your favorite facilities, leave reviews, and receive personalized recommendations

2. What should I do if I encounter incorrect information or have trouble using the website?

If you encounter any incorrect information or have trouble using the website, please contact our customer support team. We'll do our best to address any issues and ensure that the website is running smoothly for all users.

3. .How accurate are the opening hours listed on the website?

We strive to keep the opening hours of facilities up-to-date, but it's always a good idea to double-check with the facility directly, especially around holidays or during unusual circumstances.

4. Can I see photos of the facilities before visiting them?

Yes, many facilities have photos available on their profile pages. You can click on the photos to view them in full size and get a better idea of what the facility looks like.

5. Can I book appointments or make reservations through Facility Finder?

Some facilities may offer the option to book appointments or make reservations directly through the website. Look for buttons or links that say "Book Now" or "Make a Reservation" on the facility's profile page.
